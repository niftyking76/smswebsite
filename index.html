Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle data.

Important: This service provides PUBLIC, temporary phone numbers for DEMONSTRATION and TESTING purposes only. Do not use for sensitive, personal, banking, or permanent accounts. We are not affiliated with WhatsApp, Telegram, Google, or any third-party services.

No Signup Required

Our service does not require user registration, account creation, or login credentials. You can access virtual phone numbers and receive SMS messages instantly without providing any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

No Personal Data Storage

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. Since no registration is required, we have no database of user accounts, passwords, or personal details. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes or share any data with third parties.

Message Handling & Auto-Deletion

SMS messages received by our virtual numbers are temporarily displayed on our website for approximately 24-48 hours to allow users to view verification codes. After this period, messages are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers. We do not maintain archives or backups of SMS content.

Public Visibility Disclaimer

Warning: All phone numbers provided through our free service are PUBLIC and SHARED among all users. Any SMS message received by these numbers is visible to anyone visiting our website. Do not use these numbers for receiving sensitive, private, or confidential information. For private number options, please wait for our upcoming Premium service.

Third-Party Services

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to WhatsApp, Telegram, Google, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, or any other third-party service mentioned on our website. All trademarks and service marks belong to their respective owners.
